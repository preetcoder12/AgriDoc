Why Detect Plant Diseases?

Plant diseases significantly impact crop yields and plant health worldwide. Early detection is crucial for:

Preventing Crop Loss
Reducing Pesticide Use
Improving Food Security
Sustainable Farming

Without proper identification of the disease and the disease-causing agent, control measures can be ineffective, wasting time and resources while allowing plant damage to progress.

Our AI-powered detection system helps identify diseases even before symptoms become clearly visible, allowing for targeted and timely interventions.

Disease Prevention Tips
  1. Practice Good Sanitation - Remove debris and maintain clean growing areas
  2. Proper Fertilization - Keep plants healthy with balanced nutrition
  3. Inspect New Plants - Check for diseases before introducing them to your garden
  4. Warm Soil - Plant when soil has reached optimal temperature
  5. Crop Rotation - Alternate plant types to prevent pathogen buildup
  6. Air Circulation - Space plants properly to reduce humidity
  7. Prune Regularly - Remove diseased stems and foliage promptly
  8. Water Management - Water at the base and avoid overhead watering
