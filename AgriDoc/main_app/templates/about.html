About AgriDoc

Revolutionizing plant disease detection through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

AgriDoc is dedicated to helping farmers and gardeners protect their crops by providing fast, accurate, and accessible plant disease detection. We believe that early detection is the key to preventing crop loss and ensuring food security for communities worldwide.

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced agricultural technology, making it available to everyone from small-scale gardeners to large commercial farms.

What We Do

AgriDoc uses state-of-the-art deep learning and computer vision technology to identify plant diseases from images. Simply upload a photo of your plant's leaves, and our AI engine will analyze it to detect potential diseases or health issues.

Key Features:

  • Fast Detection: Get results in seconds, not days
  • High Accuracy: Powered by advanced CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) models
  • Multiple Plant Support: Detects diseases across 14+ different plant species
  • Detailed Information: Provides disease descriptions and treatment recommendations
  • Supplement Recommendations: Suggests appropriate supplements and fertilizers
  • Easy to Use: No technical knowledge required - just upload and analyze

Our Technology

AgriDoc is built using cutting-edge machine learning technologies:

Deep Learning Computer Vision CNN Architecture Python & PyTorch Cloud-Based

Our model has been trained on thousands of images of healthy and diseased plants, enabling it to recognize patterns and symptoms that might be invisible to the untrained eye. The system continuously learns and improves to provide better accuracy over time.
